Curry Chicken Roti

Chicken curry typically starts with whole spices, a sauce is then made with onions, ginger, garlic, and tomatoes, and powdered spices. Bones-in or out is made of pieces of chicken, then the chicken is added to the sauce and simmered until cooked. The most popular way of eaten Curry Chicken is typically with Roti skin(s). Hence the name “Curry Chicken Roti” or “Chicken Curry Roti.””

Peanut punch

Peanut punch is a beverage that is popular in the Caribbean. The main ingredients of this drink is peanut butter, milk and sugar. However, variations occur whereby regular milk is often replaced or added to the mix, spices, corn flakes, Angostura bitters, glucose powder and quite often granola. The drink is often regarded by some to be an aphrodisiac.

Pholourie

Pholourie, also spelled phulourie or phoulourie, is a snack food commonly eaten in Trinidad and Tobago. Pholourie is also known in Guyana and Suriname. It consists of fried, spiced dough balls and it is served with tamarind sauce.
